Dutch Juggling Convention 2025

Location

The Dutch Juggling Convention takes place each year at a different location in the Netherlands. This year the Dutch Juggling Convention takes place in Waalwijk, in sports hall de Zeine!

The address is Olympiaweg 12A, 5143 NA, Waalwijk.

A cozy place where we have all the space to juggle, workshops or just play a board game. During the Dutch Juggling Convention you can camp at the campsite.

This year the campsite is just a few minutes away from the sports hall.

Keep an eye on the website for further updates on the campsite and festival location!

Directions

Your journey to the NJF is about to begin! Below are some sample best travel options. But to be sure, always check your own navigation system or the 9292 trip planner when traveling by public transportation.

Every seat is a spot for an extra juggler! Are you coming by car? Then we encourage carpooling. Once your registration is received, we will send you a link for the NJF WhatsApp group. Here you can ask or offer a carpool spot.

By car follow the A27 from Utrecht towards Breda and take the exit to the A59 towards Waalwijk. Take exit Waalwijk-Centrum and follow the signs to the Olympiaweg.

You can park in the parking spaces near Sporthal de Zeine. Are these spaces full? No worries, you can also park your car along the Olympiaweg.

ðŸš‚ Public Transportation

Travel by train to Station 's-Hertogenbosch or Tilburg Centraal. From these stations you can take a bus to Waalwijk. Keep in mind that these buses often also go to amusement park the Efteling and therefore it can be crowded during holidays and weekends.

From Station 's-Hertogenbosch:

Take bus 301 towards Tilburg via the Efteling. Get off at bus stop RKC Stadion, Waalwijk. From here it is a 10-minute walk to the NJF.

From Tilburg Central:

You have two options:

  1. Take bus 301 towards 's-Hertogenbosch via the Efteling and get off at bus station Vredesplein, Waalwijk. From here you walk 11 minutes to the NJF.
  2. Take bus 136 towards 's-Hertogenbosch via Waalwijk and get off at Bus stop Putstraat, Waalwijk. From here it is only a 6 minute walk to the NJF.

Always check your trip in advance with the 9191-app for the most up-to-date routes and departure times.

ðŸš² Bicycle

Nice and easy and good for the environment. Are you from Waalwijk or a neighboring municipality? Then bike to the NJF quickly and easily. Unicycles count too, of course.

Tip: Coming from further away? Take your unicycle on the train or buy a bike ticket and travel to Tilburg or 's-Hertogenbosch. From here, follow the bicycle junctions to Waalwijk. A beautiful route along, among others, National Park De Loonse en Drunense Duinen. From Tilburg you cycle in about 45 minutes, and from 's-Hertogenbosch in about 50 minutes.
