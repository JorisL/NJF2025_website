Ticket info

Permission Letter

Are you under 18 and want to participate in the NJF? Then you must have a permission letter with you signed by your parent or guardian. This can be downloaded here.

Support tickets

Also this year we offer support tickets, a special kind of ticket that contributes to keeping the festival accessible and affordable for everyone. Also, with this ticket you contribute to an even more special and awesome festival, this would for example allow us to book just that one awesome gala show artist. You can add the support prices when ordering your ticket. You can choose a contribution of €15, €25 or €50.

Day tickets

Day tickets are sold at the door. With a day ticket you have access to the festival grounds all day until midnight (00:00). Day tickets do not include breakfast. You can choose day tickets with or without dinner. Day tickets do not include admission to the gala show. Tickets for the gala show are sold separately.

Day tickets for youth circuses

If you want to visit the Dutch Juggling Convention for a day with your youth circus, we have good news! This year we are offering a special discount for youth circuses. To be considered, your group must consist of at least five participants: four youth under 17 and one supervisor. For groups of ten participants or more, a second chaperone may accompany you. One application per day can be made per youth circus. Would you like to take advantage of this discount? Then email 2025@nederlandsjongleerfestival.nl to request tickets.

Spectator tickets

As an attendant or spectator you can join us with a spectator ticket. This ticket gives you access to the sports canteen of Sporthal de Zeine, where you can relax with a drink from the bar. Participants under the age of 16 must always have an attendant present, so you are expected to stay in the sports hall. Feel free to bring a book or laptop and enjoy the cozy atmosphere. Through the large windows in the cafeteria you can see what is happening in the juggling hall. This spectator ticket can be picked up for free when arriving at the convention site.

Important to know:

  • Spectator tickets are available if the participant is at least 12 years old and can juggle independently. This is important because juggling is an activity where many objects fly through the air, and we want to maintain a safe environment for all visitors.
  • If the participant is younger than 12 years old or finds it difficult to train independently, we ask an adult to purchase a day ticket. With a day ticket you have access to the venue and can juggle or support together as needed.

Gala show

All tickets for multiple days include a free ticket for the gala show on saturday (except for the Thursday+Friday ticket). The Saturday day-ticket is excluding a gala show ticket. This can be bought separately.
