Dutch Juggling Convention 2025

Save the date: May 29 - June 1 2025
The Dutch Juggling Festival, also known as the NJF, is the largest annual Dutch festival for jugglers and other circus enthusiasts. The festival takes place during the Ascension weekend. During this four-day festival, you can learn new tricks, watch shows and, above all, juggle a lot. This year, the Dutch Juggling Festival is coming to Waalwijk!
